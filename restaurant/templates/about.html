About Us

Based in Alexandria, Egypt, Little Lemon is a Mediterranean restaurant, focused on traditional recipes served with a modern twist.

The chefs draw inspiration from Egyptian, Syrian, Turkish, Greek, and Italian culture and have a menu of 12-15 items that they rotate seasonally.

The restaurant has a rustic and relaxed atmosphere with moderate prices, making it a popular place for a meal any time of the day.

Little Lemon is owned by two Italian brothers, Mario and Adrian, who moved to the United States to pursue their shared dream of owning a restaurant.

To craft the menu, Our Chefs rely on family recipes and their experience as chefs in Egypt and other mediterranean countries.
